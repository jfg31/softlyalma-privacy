Privacy Policy

Softly Alma  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jose Flores ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in connection with the Softly Alma mobile application ("App").

1. Information I Do Not Collect

I do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use it.

2. Anonymous Usage Data

The App logs anonymous, non-identifiable events (such as which screen you are viewing or when a piece of content is shared). These events contain no personal information and are used solely to understand how the App is used in aggregate so I can improve it.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain device information and use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. This data is collected by Google, not by me. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

The App stores your display preferences (such as wallpaper and font choices) locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect any information from children.
